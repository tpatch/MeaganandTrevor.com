Our Story

The Beginning

On Saturday, December 5, Meagan went out with some friends wearing her ugly Christmas sweater. Trevor and his friends also went out that night. When Meagan and Trevor first met that night, he teased her ugly sweater. They talked about dogs, Christmas, and ugly sweaters before Trevor finally asked for her phone number.

The First Year

After just a few weeks of dating, Meagan and Trevor rarely had a day they didn't spend together. Trevor's parents visited Ames to meet Meagan, and Trevor drove to Sioux City over spring break to meet Meagan's parents. Trevor graduated and decided to stay in Ames, and Meagan began her final year of college. Through new jobs and classes, they continued to spend as much time as possible together.

A Busy Spring

The next year, Meagan and Trevor had a busy spring. Meagan was graduating from ISU and wanted to pursue a nursing degree at South Dakota State. Trevor had barely even seen South Dakota, but unable to see Meagan leave for a year, he decided to follow her. Within the span of a few weeks, they enrolled Meagan in nursing school, found Trevor a job, found a new apartment, packed everything up and moved.

Sioux Falls

Trevor and Meagan soon settled into their new lives in South Dakota. Meagan studied hard at SDSU for her nursing degree while Trevor began his career as a web developer at Lawrence & Schiller. They found some new friends through school and work, and in their free time went out to explore Sioux Falls or traveled to Sioux City to see Meagan's family. After a busy year of nursing school, Meagan graduated and took a position at Sanford Health.

The Proposal

In February 2013, Meagan and Trevor went out for an early Valentines day celebration. After a nice dinner Trevor convinced Meagan to go for a walk through Falls Park, which was difficult due to the freezing temperature that night. The falls were lit up and when they stopped to take photos, Trevor finally got the courage to get down on one knee and ask Meagan to marry him. Meagan said 'Yes', and after their initial excitement, they ran back to the warm car to call their friends and family.
